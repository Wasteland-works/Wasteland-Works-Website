About Wasteland Works

Wasteland Works is a collaborative workshop dedicated to designing, building, and experimenting with technology.

We enjoy tackling projects that combine software, electronics, fabrication, design, and problem solving.

Some ideas become useful tools. Some become products. Some become valuable learning experiences.

Our goal is simple: build interesting things, learn new skills, and share what we create.

Areas of Interest

  • Software Development
  • Arduino & Embedded Systems
  • Electronics Design
  • PCB Development
  • 3D Design & Printing
  • Mechanical Prototyping
  • Experimental Projects

The Team

Wasteland Works is powered by a small group of builders, makers, designers, programmers and experimenters who enjoy turning ideas into reality.
